How to Join Project Phoenix Roleplay

+

Step 1: Join Our Discord Server

Click the "Join Discord" button above to enter our community. Once there, Head to channels and roles and get yourself the After the Flash role to access its channels.

1
+

Step 2: Read the Rules and Lore

Familiarize yourself with our community guidelines and the rich backstory of Project Phoenix on our website.

2
+

Step 3: Create Your Character

Use the character template in our Discord server to design your unique persona. Head to the official After the Flash game to stylize that persona in the game. Our moderators will guide you through the process if help is needed.

3
+

Step 4: Get Your Character Approved

Submit your character for approval in the #character-approval channel. Once approved, our team will assign your character to a faction that makes the most sense for your character and the plot.

4
+

Step 5: Jump Into the Action

Wait for a roleplay session to start in the #events channel and start your adventure in the post-apocalyptic world of Project Phoenix!
