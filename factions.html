Factions of Project Phoenix

Choose your allegiance and shape the future of the Sunrise Islands

USRF (United States Reclamation Force)

"Order from Chaos"

Military faction aiming to secure Project Phoenix to restore pre-war order and stabilize the region under USRF control.

Bandit Gangs

"Power through Anarchy"

Ruthless groups seeking to use Project Phoenix to establish dominance over the island and enforce their rule through fear and power.

The Resistance

"Hope and Rebuild"

A civilian group fighting for survival, hoping to utilize Project Phoenix to protect their communities and rebuild civilization from the ashes.

The Alumni

"Knowledge is Power"

Tech-savvy group interested in unlocking the technological potential of Project Phoenix to advance humanity's future and ensure their faction's superiority in technology.
