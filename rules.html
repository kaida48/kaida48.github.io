Roleplay Rules

Welcome to the wasteland of Project Phoenix. These rules are your survival guide in this post-apocalyptic world. Follow them to ensure a fair and immersive experience for all survivors.

Foreword

Participants in the roleplay should be well-acquainted with the rules outlined in this document to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone. Not following these rules may lead to exclusion from the roleplay. It's important that all participants understand and respect the guidelines to maintain the integrity and immersion of the roleplaying environment. By joining the roleplay, you agree to these rules and acknowledge the consequences of breaking them.

Roleplay Basics

Roleplay is the authentic playing of a fictional character in our post-apocalyptic world. Every action affects your character's survival and story.

The Terms OOC and IC

IC (In Character)

You're in the wasteland, fighting for survival.

OOC (Out of Character)

You're back in the real world.

Prohibited Actions

Metagaming

Using out-of-character knowledge in the wasteland.

Fail-RP

Actions that defy the logic of our post-apocalyptic world.

R-D-M

Intentional killing of players without a valid roleplay background.

Power-RP

Roleplay where the other party is not given a chance to react.

E-RP

Sexual actions within roleplay.

RP-Escape

Deliberate escape from an ongoing RP situation.

Extreme-RP

Actions such as racism, hate crimes, rape, excessive torture, bomb threats, bombings, suicide attacks or more extreme terrorist acts, stalking, or dismemberment.

Copbaiting

Deliberate distraction of police officers, preventing them from doing their job.

Gambo-RP

Excessive, disproportionate, or unnecessary use of weapons and gunfights.

Required Roleplay Elements

  • Fear-RP: The feeling of fear of a character. Everyone is afraid of something; no one is immune to everything.
  • Pain-RP: The reaction of your character to any kind of pain.
  • Realistic-Building-RP: Everything you build must be realistic and must be explainable by physical laws.

The Wasteland Dictionary

Common terms adapted for our post-apocalyptic setting:

  • AFK: Lost in the wasteland
  • Chat: Wasteland radio
  • Discord: VoiceApp
  • Health: Describes how you feel / what you feel / if something hurts
  • Inventory: Backpack
  • Lag: Headache / Microsleep / Inattention (use only if absolutely necessary)
  • Roblox Name: Nickname
  • Voice System (or Discord): Vocal cords (use only if absolutely necessary)

General Rules

  1. IC and OOC must be separated.
  2. The prohibited actions listed above are not allowed in roleplay.

Character Rules

  1. Each character must be unique and must not reflect any fictional or real person.
  2. The name of a character must be chosen in the form "First name, Last name". Puns, numbers, or special characters are not allowed. Double names are also not possible.
  3. The minimum age of the character is 16 years. This means that the character automatically reaches adulthood with us.
  4. Each player must use a character that is "human".

New Life Rule

  1. Each player must use a character that is "human".
