The World of Project Phoenix

Explore the desolate wasteland of the Sunrise Islands

Time Period: 2160s

The game is set in the decade of the 2160s, beginning in the year 2161 - exactly 90 years after the nuclear bombs fell. This era represents a world still grappling with the long-term consequences of global devastation.

  • 90 years of post-apocalyptic adaptation and survival
  • Emerging new societies and power structures
  • Remnants of pre-war technology mixed with makeshift innovations
  • Ongoing struggles for resources, territory, and survival

Location: Sunrise Islands

The Sunrise Islands are a fictional archipelago located slightly north of mainland Ohio in the post-apocalyptic remnants of Lake Erie. Once a thriving vacation destination, it's now a desolate wasteland in the aftermath of a devastating nuclear strike. The islands are home to scattered survivors and various factions vying for control and resources.

  • Two main islands in close proximity, forming the heart of the archipelago
  • Two smaller surrounding islands, each with its own unique challenges and opportunities
  • Treacherous waters filled with mutated aquatic life from the irradiated Lake Erie

Environment

The Sunrise Islands present a diverse and dangerous landscape, shaped by nuclear devastation and years of neglect. Survivors must navigate through various hazardous terrains:

  • Irradiated Zones

    Areas of extreme radiation, deadly to the unprepared but rich in valuable pre-war technology.

  • Ruined Cities

    Once-great metropolises now reduced to skeletal structures, home to scavengers and mutants alike.

  • Underground Shelters

    Remnants of pre-war survival efforts, these bunkers offer refuge but often hide dark secrets.

  • Mutated Wildlife

    Animals and plants warped by radiation, creating new and terrifying species.

  • Scarce Resources

    Clean water, uncontaminated food, and functional technology are rare and highly sought after.

  • Unstable Weather

    Unpredictable and extreme weather patterns, from acid rain to radioactive storms.

Post-Apocalyptic Society

In the wake of global devastation, new social structures have emerged:

  • Fractured communities struggling for survival
  • Emergence of powerful factions with conflicting ideologies
  • Barter system replacing traditional currency
  • Knowledge of pre-war technology becoming a valuable commodity
  • Constant struggle between cooperation and conflict
