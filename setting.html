The World of Project Phoenix

Welcome to the desolate wasteland of the Sunrise Islands, where survival is a daily struggle and hope is a rare commodity.

Time Period

2164

The world is engulfed in the flames of World War 3, a nuclear conflict that has reshaped the face of the planet.

Location

Sunrise Islands

Once a thriving archipelago, now a desolate wasteland in the aftermath of a devastating nuclear strike. The islands are home to scattered survivors and various factions vying for control and resources.

Environment

  • Irradiated zones
  • Ruined cities
  • Underground shelters
  • Mutated wildlife
  • Scarce resources
