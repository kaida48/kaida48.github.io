The World of Project Phoenix

The Sunrise Islands: A Post-Apocalyptic Haven

In the year 2164, the Sunrise Islands, nestled in Lake Erie, Ohio, have become a desolate wasteland following a recent nuclear strike amidst an ongoing nuclear World War 3. Once vibrant and bustling, these islands are now a barren landscape filled with scattered survivors and various factions vying for control and survival.

The Aftermath

The nuclear strike has left the islands in ruins, with radiation pockets, mutated wildlife, and treacherous terrain. Survivors must navigate this dangerous landscape while battling the elements and each other.

Factions of the Sunrise Islands

The Preservers

A group dedicated to salvaging and preserving pre-war technology and knowledge.

The Mutant Collective

A community of radiation-altered humans fighting for acceptance and survival.

The New Dawn

Idealists aiming to rebuild society from the ground up with sustainable practices.

Key Locations

  • The Glowing Crater: Epicenter of the nuclear strike, now a dangerous but resource-rich area.
  • Haven City: The largest remaining settlement, a beacon of hope in the wasteland.
  • The Undercity: A network of pre-war bunkers and tunnels, home to various factions.
